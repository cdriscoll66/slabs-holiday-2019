Meeting Merriment

This holiday we're giving the gift of play.

Meetings can be stressful. Complex dynamics, competing agendas, advanced social calculus. It can be a nerve wracking experience. We invented a game to help cut that tension. To alleviate the nerves. To bring whimsy to the workplace. Because the world could always use a little more play, especially in the most serious of spaces— the modern corporate meeting.

The game is simple

  • Select a word for each person on your team before a meeting.
  • Everyone tries to slide their word into conversation as seamlessly as possible. The goal is to be as subtle as possible so no one would ever know, except the people on your team.
  • Everyone who uses their word wins.

The game is a way to keep your team on their toes, have a little fun, and build a shared moment. Below you'll find a starter pack of words so you can play our game in your own meetings. Some are beginner words, like “serendipity" or “dollop". Others are a bit more challenging, like "Billy Joel in a bungalow" or "post post-modern." If you want the advanced version, you'll have to reach out.

Happy holidays from Sylvain Labs. And here's to the merriest of meetings in 2020.
